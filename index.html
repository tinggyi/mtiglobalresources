Scholarship Types

🎓

University Scholarships

Awarded by universities based on the student’s overall profile.

Typical value: 5%–50% tuition fee reduction

⭐

Merit Scholarships

For students with strong academic results and consistent performance.

Typical value: 10%–50% tuition fee reduction

📚

Subject Scholarships

Available for selected courses such as Business, Engineering, or IT.

Typical value: 10%–30% tuition fee reduction

🌏

Regional/ASEAN Scholarships

Offered to students from ASEAN countries by certain universities.

Typical value: 5%–20% tuition fee reduction

💰

Early Payment / Tuition Discounts

Fee reductions given for early confirmation or early tuition payment.

Typical value: 5%–10% tuition fee reduction

Working Holiday Pathways

A practical overseas gap year to gain work experience and clarity before committing to long-term study or career paths.

What is a Working Holiday?

A government-approved visa that allows young adults to live and work overseas legally for a fixed period, usually 6–12 months, depending on the country.

Who is this for?

  • Students or fresh graduates
  • Those unsure what degree or career path to commit to
  • Individuals seeking overseas exposure before long-term study
  • Anyone planning future study or work pathways
  • Young adults aged 18–30 (or 35 for some countries)

Why Does It Matter?

A structured gap year to gain real-world experience and clarity before committing to a full degree or further studies.

Popular Destinations

  • Australia: High wages, wide range of short-term jobs
  • United Kingdom: Work experience with access to Europe
  • Ireland: English-speaking, strong job demand, EU exposure
  • New Zealand: Seasonal work, balanced lifestyle
  • Canada: Multicultural environment, structured work options
